Generation Epsilon

2075–2089

Generation Epsilon will live in a post-scarcity society driven by limitless clean energy and AI-managed ecosystems. Poverty and pollution will be relics of the past as humanity learns to live in harmony with nature and technology. They will explore deep space, create digital worlds, and use nanotechnology to extend life and health. This generation will redefine what progress means — not domination, but balance, knowledge, and peace.
