Generation Gamma

2045–2059

Generation Gamma will live during the age of interplanetary expansion. With human colonies on Mars and the Moon, their worldview will stretch beyond Earth. Space travel, quantum computing, and neural connectivity will shape their daily lives. They’ll grow up exploring both real and virtual universes — pioneers of a truly cosmic civilization. To them, distance will be irrelevant; curiosity will be infinite.
