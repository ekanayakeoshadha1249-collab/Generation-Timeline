Generation Zeta

2090–2109

Generation Zeta will witness humanity fully embrace interstellar life. Their society will span multiple planets, united by instantaneous communication networks powered by quantum entanglement. Physical borders will no longer matter — instead, culture, knowledge, and art will connect civilizations across the stars. They’ll grow up learning both Earth and Martian history as part of a single human heritage. Generation Zeta will redefine what “home” means — not a planet, but a shared galaxy.
