Generation Eta

2110–2129

Generation Eta will live in an era where consciousness itself can be duplicated, shared, and preserved. Digital immortality will become a reality — people will exist simultaneously in physical and virtual forms. Ethics, identity, and individuality will evolve beyond recognition as humans learn to coexist with their digital twins. Generation Eta will explore what it truly means to be alive, redefining the boundaries of humanity.
