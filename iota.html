Generation Iota

2150–2169

Generation Iota will represent the synthesis of human, AI, and alien intelligence. By this era, communication with extraterrestrial life may reshape philosophy and science completely. The Iota generation will view themselves as part of a universal civilization — learning, sharing, and evolving alongside other sentient beings. Their mission will be to unite intelligence across the cosmos, bringing harmony beyond Earth.
