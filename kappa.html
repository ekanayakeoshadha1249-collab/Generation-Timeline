Generation Kappa

2170–2190+

Generation Kappa may become the bridge between humanity and eternity. Their existence will be defined not by survival, but by purpose — exploring the universe, preserving consciousness, and guiding new forms of life. They will inherit a universe where energy, matter, and thought are interchangeable. Generation Kappa will stand as the stewards of cosmic wisdom, carrying the legacy of every generation before them into infinity.
