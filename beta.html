Generation Beta

2030–2044

Generation Beta is expected to inherit a planet transformed by artificial intelligence and environmental innovation. They will grow up in smart cities powered by renewable energy, surrounded by autonomous systems and bio-technology. Education will be deeply immersive — taught through holographic classrooms and virtual mentors. With climate recovery at the center of global efforts, this generation will live in a cleaner, smarter, and more interconnected Earth. Their values will blend technology with empathy, aiming to heal the relationship between people and planet.
