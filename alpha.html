Generation Alpha

2013–2029

Generation Alpha is the first generation born fully into the 21st century — children of the digital revolution. Growing up surrounded by AI, robotics, and augmented reality, they are hyper-connected and technologically fluent from birth. Their education relies heavily on immersive experiences and personalized AI tutors, shaping an era of intelligence and creativity. They value sustainability, diversity, and global collaboration more than any generation before them. Generation Alpha will define what it truly means to be human in a world shared with intelligent machines.
